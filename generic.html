About Me

DATA ANALYST

Hi, my name is Linda. All through my childhood, Mathematics was my favorite subject as I loved solving problems.

During my High School, we had Computer classes, which gave me the basic knowledge of Computer System. These classes invoked my curiosity to learn more about Information Technology. Which led me to pursue Bachelors Degree in Electrical/Computer Engineering to further explore and enhance my problem solving skills. Machine Language and Big Data Analytics were my favorite subjects during Bachelors. I was curious about Data Analysis and insights it could provide.

Therefore, in 2022, I decided to explore my passion in Data and pursue a career in Data Analytics.
